About Us

Our Mission

MAITRI aims to show the Love and care in action by ministering to the needy man kind irrespective of any differences, in meeting their Sociological, Physical, Psychological, Intellectual and Spiritual needs and empowering and enabling them to become channels of blessing to others through wholistic approach.

In order to accomplish the Vision statement, MAITRI wished to run Hospitals, Welfare Centers for deserted, Destitute and desperate children, Women, and Aged people to lead a socially and economically independent dignified life and shine as a proud citizen of the great free land.

Old Age Home

Here's the revised version with the self-finance detail included in the first line: Based on a self-finance model, MAITRI Society runs this Old Age Home with a mission to care for the elderly who are left without love or support in society, ensuring that senior citizens live their final years with dignity, security, and emotional well-being.

The home provides nutritious food, secure shelter, daily care, medical assistance, counseling, empowerment support, free legal aid, and recreational facilities. Every service is designed to nurture the elderly and support their physical and emotional needs.

MAITRI strives to make this home a safe and respectful environment, where the elderly can live their later years with happiness, peace, and a renewed sense of hope.

Sakthi Sadan

Sakthi Sadan is a government-supported initiative for women in distress, funded under a 60% Central and 40% State government cost-sharing scheme. The program, previously known as Swadhar Greh, is now officially referred to as Shakti Sadan.

This shelter is designed to support women who are victims of violence, abuse, or difficult social circumstances. It offers a safe and secure environment for women who lack social and economic supportтАФsuch as widows, destitute women, survivors of domestic violence, and women rescued from trafficking.

Sakthi Sadan aims to provide temporary residential accommodation, food, clothing, counseling, legal aid, and vocational training. The home also assists in rehabilitation, helping women become self-reliant and reintegrated into society with dignity.

Women Development

MAITRI Society is dedicated to promote holistic women development through the formation and strengthening of Self-Help Groups (SHGs). These groups serve as the foundation for womenтАЩs economic and social empowerment at the grassroots level.

Regular capacity-building programs are organized to enhance womenтАЩs skills in leadership, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and group management. These trainings equip women to manage resources effectively and make informed decisions.

Gender sensitization workshops are conducted to raise awareness about gender equity and promote inclusive community participation. These sessions address social biases and encourage women to claim their rights confidently.
